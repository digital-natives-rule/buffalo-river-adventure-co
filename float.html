Float the Bufalo!

Crystal-clear water. Towering bluffs. Unforgettable floats.

Floating Tips

  • No glass containers on the river—bring cans or reusable bottles.
  • Canoe rentals include paddles and life jackets.
  • Shuttle services are for vehicles only—you’ll paddle to your take-out and we’ll make sure your car is waiting when you get there.
  • Cell service is very limited in the area. Plan ahead and screenshot your float route if needed.
