Trails Worth Exploring

Hit the dirt. Take in the views. Breath.

Tucked away in the hills near Ponca, Bear Ridge Cabin offers the perfect home base for your next Buffalo River getaway. Designed for two, this fully furnished hideaway is made for romantic escapes, unplugged weekends, or peaceful stays surrounded by nature (and elk!).

Popular Hiking Trails

Lost Valley

A must-see trail just minutes from Boxley Ridge Cabin. Features waterfalls, caves, and bluff shelters. (1.2 miles one way)

Whitaker Point

One of the most photographed spots in Arkansas. Moderate hike with a big payoff. (3 miles round trip)

Hemmed-In Hollow

Home to the tallest waterfall between the Rockies and the Appalachians. (5.5 miles round trip from the river)

Tips for Trail Days

  • Check weather + trail conditions before heading out
  • Pack out all trash
  • Stay on marked trails
  • Watch for wildlife and enjoy from a distance
  • Cell service is limited, so plan your route ahead
