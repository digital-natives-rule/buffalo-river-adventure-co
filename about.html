About Us

Nested in the heart of the Ozarks, Buffalo River Adventure Co. is your basecamp for exploring the Buffalo National River—one of the most beautiful and rugged areas in America.

We're passionate about helping people experience the magic of the outdoors, whether it's paddling a spring-fed river or hiking a centuries-old trail cutting through cliffs, all in the truly breathtaking trails where deer roam free.

Rooted in Newton Co. AR

Our Beginnings

As a small outfitter rooted in Newton County, Arkansas, we believe celebrating this incredible natural area ensures that it’s respected. That means caring for the land, supporting local businesses like Buffalo Canoes, roads and other local work, and welcoming everyone who shares our love for wild places.

Right now, we offer cabin and canoe rentals, but we're dreaming bigger—think mountain hiking, rock climbing, and guided hikes in the future.

Join the fun!

Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a weekend paddling trip, or a deep breath of fresh air, we’re here to help you reconnect—with nature, with yourself, and with the trails ahead.
